Privacy Policy for Shibase

Last updated: December 27, 2024

1. Introduction

Shibase ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Shibase mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Audio Recordings

Our app uses the RECORD_AUDIO permission to allow users to send voice messages within the platform. Audio recordings are:

  • Recorded only when you explicitly choose to record a voice message
  • Stored temporarily on your device
  • Transmitted only to the intended recipient(s) in your conversations
  • Not used for any other purpose

2.2 Wallet Information

We integrate with Ethereum wallets through Privy authentication. We do not store your private keys or wallet credentials. Wallet addresses are used solely for:

  • User identification
  • Transaction processing
  • Platform functionality

2.3 Account Information

We collect information you provide when creating your profile, including name, bio, skills, and portfolio projects.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our Service
  • Enable communication between clients and freelancers
  • Process transactions and payments
  • Send notifications and updates

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Privy: For wallet authentication and user management
  • Ethereum Network: For blockchain transactions

  • Opt-out of certain data collection
